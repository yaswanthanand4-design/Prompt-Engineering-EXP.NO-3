Exploring the Different Types of Prompts
AIM

To study and understand different types of prompting techniques used in Artificial Intelligence systems such as Straightforward Prompts, Tabular Format Prompting, Preceding Question Prompting, and Missing Word Prompting by creating prompts for different scenarios and analyzing their outputs.

INTRODUCTION

Prompting is the method of giving instructions or questions to an AI system to generate meaningful responses. Different prompting techniques are used based on the type of information needed. Proper prompting improves the accuracy, clarity, and quality of AI-generated responses.

In this experiment, four important prompting methods are explored:

Straightforward Prompting
Tabular Format Prompting
Preceding Question Prompting
Missing Word Prompting

Each prompt type is explained with two different scenarios and suitable examples.

1. STRAIGHTFORWARD PROMPTS
Definition

Straightforward prompts directly ask the AI system to provide information or answers without any complex structure.

Scenario 1: Environmental Science
Prompt:

“Explain the importance of recycling in simple words.”

Output:

Recycling is important because it reduces waste, saves natural resources, decreases pollution, and protects the environment for future generations.

Scenario 2: Computer Technology
Prompt:

“List four advantages of using the Internet.”

Output:
Easy communication
Online learning
Fast information access
Online banking and shopping
Scenario 3: Healthcare
Prompt:

“What are the benefits of regular exercise?”

Output:

Regular exercise improves physical fitness, reduces stress, strengthens muscles, and helps maintain good health.

Scenario 4: Education
Prompt:

“Define online learning in one sentence.”

Output:

Online learning is a method of education where students learn through internet-based platforms and digital resources.

Explanation of Straightforward Prompting

Straightforward prompting is simple and direct. It is useful when quick and accurate answers are needed. These prompts are commonly used in education, customer support, and information retrieval systems.

2. TABULAR FORMAT PROMPTING
Definition

Tabular format prompting requests the AI system to provide information in a table format for easy comparison and understanding.

Scenario 1: Programming Languages
Prompt:

“Compare Python and Java programming languages in a table.”

Feature	Python	Java
Type	Interpreted Language	Compiled Language
Syntax	Simple	Complex
Speed	Moderate	Fast
Usage	AI, Data Science	Enterprise Applications

“Provide a table comparing Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.”

Feature	Wi-Fi	Bluetooth
Range	Long Distance	Short Distance

Power Usage	More	Less
Main Use	Internet Access	Device Connectivity

Explanation of Tabular Format Prompting

Tabular prompting organizes information into rows and columns. It improves readability and allows easy comparison between multiple concepts or technologies.

3. PRECEDING QUESTION PROMPTING
Definition

Preceding Question Prompting first asks a question and then requests a detailed explanation from the AI system.

Scenario 1: Climate Change
Prompt:

“Why is climate change considered a global problem? Explain the causes and effects.”

Output:

Climate change is considered a global problem because it affects weather patterns, sea levels, agriculture, and human health worldwide. Major causes include greenhouse gas emissions, deforestation, and industrial pollution. Effects include global warming, floods, droughts, and loss of biodiversity.

Scenario 2: Artificial Intelligence
Prompt:

“What is Artificial Intelligence? Explain its applications in daily life.”

Output:

Artificial Intelligence is a technology that enables machines to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence. It is used in virtual assistants, healthcare, online shopping, banking, and self-driving vehicles.

Scenario 3: Cyber Security
Prompt:

“Why is cyber security important? Describe different cyber threats.”

Output:

Cyber security is important because it protects computer systems and personal data from attacks. Common cyber threats include malware, phishing, hacking, ransomware, and identity theft.

Scenario 4: Space Technology
Prompt:

“How do satellites help communication systems? Explain briefly.”

Output:

Satellites help communication systems by transmitting television, internet, and telephone signals across long distances. They enable global communication and weather monitoring.

Explanation of Preceding Question Prompting

This prompting method encourages detailed responses. It is useful in educational systems, technical explanations, and analytical tasks where reasoning and elaboration are required.

4. MISSING WORD PROMPTING
Definition

Missing Word Prompting provides incomplete sentences where the AI must fill in the correct missing word or phrase.

Scenario 1: Geography
Prompt:

“The capital city of Australia is ____.”

Output:

Canberra

Scenario 2: Biology
Prompt:

“The process by which plants make food is called ____.”

Output:

Photosynthesis

Scenario 3: Physics
Prompt:

“The SI unit of force is ____.”

Output:

Newton

Scenario 4: Computer Science
Prompt:

“The brain of a computer is called the ____.”

Output:

CPU (Central Processing Unit)

Explanation of Missing Word Prompting

Missing word prompting is mainly used in quizzes, language learning, educational activities, and assessment systems. It helps test knowledge and understanding of concepts.
